About Me

Hi! I'm Amalia and I love all things culinary. While I'm not a professionally trained chef, I am a foodie, student, and currently the Communications Associate for the Howard Hughes Medical Institute. In my free time, I enjoy exploring the rising DC culinary scene by checking out new restaurants, trying out new recipes and watching endless hours of Tasty videos, scrolling through blogs and watching the Food Network.

How did I fall in love with food? Well, how can you not?! I've been cooking since I can remember, playing in the kitchen while my Nicaraguan grandmother made homemade gallo pinto and my mom made over 30 budnt cakes for our friends and family members during the holidays. Since then, I've explored several types of cooking, but my favorite will always be baking. I am a sugar-addict. Check out my blog below and feel free to comment, like, and share my content. Key Lime Pie
*ingredients*
  • 1 premade graham cracker pie crust


  • 6 egg yolks


  • 2- 14-ounce cans condensed milk


  • 3/4 cup freshly squeezed key lime juice






    • *directions*
  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees


  • Whisk together the egg yolks, condensed milk & key lime juice


  • Pour the filling into the premade graham cracker pie crust


  • Bake for 25-35 minutes or until the filling is set


  • Let the pie cool and refridgerate for 3 hours


  • Cut, serve & enjoy!
